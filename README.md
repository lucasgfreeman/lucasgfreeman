Welcome to my portfolio! From maps and planispheres to almanacs, dictionaries, and charts, I’ve been studying data for as long as I can remember. My earliest memory involves anagramming a 🛑 sign on the way to kindergarten. The fact that my brain instinctively explored permutations shows the early curiosity I had for seeing patterns, rearranging symbols, and finding structure. Now, after twenty years in the veterinary world (with side quests in literary, psychology, and fitness), I am channeling the wealth of the data exploration I've encountered into a more data-driven approach to analysis and decision making.

My main areas of interest are:
- 🐶 Veterinary and animal science
- 🏺 Ancient and medieval history
- 🧠 Psychology, neurology, philosophy, and religion
- 📚 Books and bookselling
- 🏰 Fantasy and historical fiction
- 🥗 Health, fitness, and nutrition
- 💵 Finance
- ⚪ Minimalism
